# Lookout — Standalone Usage Sensor

Added `suite-design/lookout/lookout.ps1`: a sensor-only PowerShell 5.1 script
that polls the undocumented `oauth/usage` endpoint, writes
`state/usage.json`, and creates/removes `state/ALERT` and `state/PAUSE`
against configurable thresholds. It never launches, kills, resumes, or
controls anything - signal-only, for a future supervisor to read.

Proven against real, live usage (no mocks): started at a 41% baseline,
armed `-PauseAt 42`, and had a human generate real usage elsewhere until the
5-hour window crossed 42%. The Lookout raised `state/PAUSE` on its own;
detection latency was ~2 seconds. Restarting at the real defaults
(`-AlertAt 80 -PauseAt 93`) let the Lookout clear `PAUSE` on its own too,
confirming the removal path with real data.

`suite-design/lookout/state/` is gitignored (runtime-only).
